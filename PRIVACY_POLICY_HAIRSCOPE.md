# HairScope — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 10, 2026**
**Effective Date: April 10, 2026**

---

## Introduction

Welcome to HairScope (the "App"), developed and published by **ForgeTech (Haikou) Co., Ltd.** ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

HairScope is a **standalone, offline-only tool** designed to help you track your hair health over time. **All data stays on your device. We do not operate any servers, do not collect any personal information, and do not transmit any data off your device.** This Privacy Policy explains our practices in detail.

---

## Our Core Privacy Principle

> **HairScope is a 100% offline application. No data — including photos, analysis results, or personal settings — ever leaves your device. There are no user accounts, no cloud storage, no analytics, and no tracking.**

---

## Information We Collect

### We do NOT collect any information.

HairScope does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data with us or any third party. Specifically:

- ❌ **No server communication** — The App does not connect to any external servers.
- ❌ **No photo uploads** — All photos remain exclusively on your device.
- ❌ **No analytics or tracking** — We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking tools.
- ❌ **No advertising** — The App contains no ads and no ad-related tracking.
- ❌ **No user accounts** — There is no sign-up, login, or account system.
- ❌ **No personal identifiers** — We do not collect your name, email, phone number, location, or any other personal information.

---

## Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is created and stored **locally on your device only**, using on-device storage (IndexedDB). This data never leaves your device:

| Data Type | Purpose | Storage Location |
|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| Photos (hairline & crown) | Visual hair tracking | Device only (IndexedDB) |
| Analysis results & scores | On-device hair health assessment | Device only (IndexedDB) |
| Nickname & avatar | Personalization | Device only (IndexedDB) |
| Age range & gender | Context for on-device analysis | Device only (IndexedDB) |
| Self-assessment questionnaire | Personal risk evaluation | Device only (IndexedDB) |
| Detection history & trends | Progress tracking over time | Device only (IndexedDB) |
| App settings & preferences | User customization | Device only (IndexedDB) |

You have full control over this data at all times. You can delete all data from within the App's settings, or by uninstalling the App.

---

## Camera & Photo Access

HairScope requests camera permission to capture photos of your hairline and crown area for analysis.

- The camera is used **only when you actively initiate a scan**.
- Photos are processed **entirely on your device** using on-device computer vision models.
- **No photos are uploaded, transmitted, or shared** with any server or third party.
- You can revoke camera permission at any time in your device's Settings.

---

## On-Device Analysis

HairScope performs hair analysis using **on-device computer vision and machine learning models**:

- **Face Mesh (MediaPipe)** — Used locally to detect facial landmarks for accurate hairline positioning.
- **Image segmentation** — Used locally to analyze crown coverage and density.
- **Smart Summary Engine** — Generates personalized insights using deterministic on-device logic. No AI cloud API is involved.

All processing happens on your device. No data is sent to any external service.

### Non-Biometric Use

The App does **not** use facial or image data for:
- Identity verification or facial recognition
- Biometric authentication
- User profiling or behavioral tracking

All image processing is used solely for hair appearance analysis.

## Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- **You control your data.** You can delete all analysis records, photos, and personal settings at any time from the App's Settings page.
- **Uninstalling the App** will remove all locally stored data (IndexedDB and related app storage).
- **We retain nothing.** Because we do not operate servers or collect data, there is no server-side data to retain or delete.

> **Note:** The device identifier used for Apple In-App Purchase verification is managed by Apple's StoreKit framework and stored in iOS Keychain. This identifier may persist after app uninstallation. It is managed by Apple and is not accessible to us.
